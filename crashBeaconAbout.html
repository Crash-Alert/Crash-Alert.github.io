About Crash Beacon

What is Crash Beacon?

Crash Beacon utilizes the built-in accelerometer in android phones to detect when phones experience extreme forces such as those during a plane crash. When the force experienced by the phone exceeds a certain threshold, the Geolocation as well as an S.O.S. signal are sent to a server where response teams are able to track the trajectory of a plane as it is descending. Thereby, creating an accurate search area for the crashed plane.

Application to Real World Events

The team was inspired by recent world events such as the lost Mylasian Airlines Flight. They wanted to make a difference by helping the families of those involved in plane crashes get closure and possibly even save the lives of many loved ones.
